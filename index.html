What is DSC Esa Unggul?

DSC Esa Unggul is Study Group for student from any academic background, and majors in their undergraduate or graduate term to share experiences and learn together to be a Future tech Ready by collaborating on solving Local Problem. DSC member will build their personal and profesional network and get get Access to Google Developer resource.

Learn More

Global DSC Goal

Our goal is to create impact. Impact students, and empower them to impact their communities through technology.

Global DSC Vision

Developer Student Clubs is the chosen program for students in universities wanting to grow as developers.
